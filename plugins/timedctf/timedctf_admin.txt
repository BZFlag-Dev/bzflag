----------------------------------------------------------
Timed CTF Commands Available To Admins:
----------------------------------------------------------
Admin only:
"ctfcaptime iii" - change cap time to iii
"ctfcaptimeoff" - disable timed CTF. 
"ctfcaptimeon" - enable timed CTF. 
"ctfcaptimestatus" - return status of timedctf 
"fairctfoff" - disable the fair ctf function. 
"fairctfon" - enable the fair ctf function.
